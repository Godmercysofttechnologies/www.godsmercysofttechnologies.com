Gods Mercy Soft Technologies is one of the leading iT [Infomation Technology] companies in Uganda with head quarters in Pakwatch.
Founded in the year 2019 to proactively identify new areas of technology that can be leveraged for efficiency, productivity and service delivery to individuals and instituions in Uganda and beyond.
We develop and deploy innovative software solutions with a team of young, result-driven professionals who are committed to excellence and global standards. We specialize in providing cutting edge solutions that enable institutions and organizations improve on service delivery, and the entire management of enterprise operations.
We have been providing software solutions and iT management support since 2019 and currently supports a number of clients across Uganda. Our customers operate in many industry sectors including education, finance, retail, accounting, sales and corporate..
