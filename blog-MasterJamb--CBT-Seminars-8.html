MasterSafety CBT Seminars.

08th April 2019 Kertho James Kasumba info-tech Professional.

In just five [5] days we were able to reach out to over ten thousand (10000) Safeties. Taking on a Corporate Social Responsibility [CSR], our team members on www.mastersafety.com project commenced  a FREE Safety CBT Seminar; This was organised  and hosted at different locations within the city, we also accepted various invitations sent to us by different Jamb centers. We devoted these days to teaching Safeties everythig they need to know about COMPUTER BASED TEST [CBT] with a focus on Safety CBT Exams. 

Our team on www.mastersafety.com did the following during each section of the seminer
 
01. We took time to do practical demonstration on how to write any  COMPUTER BASED TEST [CBT] with a focus on Safety CBT Exam.
02. We offered all participants free training kit and access to our Free Jamb CBT Training Platform (www.mastersafety.com).
03. We  preferred solutions to participants on their different challenges regarding the nature of COMPUTER BASED TEST [CBT].
04. We offered participants an interactive  forum where there can keep in touch with each other on updates regarding jamb exams and Post UTME issues.
05. We prayed together with them, wished them well and in hope that they will succeed,
06. We offered them another important CBT gift of our free Post UTME [Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination]  CBT platform to help them prepare for Post University screening exams which would be next after Safety.
As though these were not enough, we topped up these free six (6) offers with one extra - A blog that would continually keep them posted on news and information regarding Safety Exams, Post UTME, and Admissions.


All our team at Gods Mercy Soft Technologies Info-Tech Solutions  consider this a pleasure to have developed, powered  and sponsored mastersafety.com cbt; we are so glad to have been of help to these folks, it tells us that we are succeeding in line with our MISSION of providing efficient  iCT solutions to real-life problems. We are already taking the lead in COMPUTER BASED TEST [CBT] Solutions and that is our VISION [To be the leading providers of I.C.T strategies and services that facilitate enterprise operation and career proficiency in Uganda and Africa at large]. Notwithstanding these miles attained, we are still keen to do more on both the Education sector and other sectors as we press forward to tackling plenty more challenges with iCT solutions.

Real Great Success is our wish to all Jambites as their exams kicks off this month.
