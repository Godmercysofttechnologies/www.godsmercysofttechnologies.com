MasterSafety Free CBT Software.

12th February 2019 Gods Mercy Soft Technologies info-tech Professional.

Gods Mercy Soft Technologies  Computer Based Test [CBT] software application popularly known as MasterSafety is now free.
MasterSafety CBT software is a computer Based Test software application specifically designed for Safeties. It was designed and launched in the year 2019 with  database of Safety past questions and answers to enable students preparing for safety examination a thorough CBT rehearsal before going for Safety exams.

MasterSafety software was launched in the year 2019 with  3 unique versions -  
01. Online version accessible with the use of scratch off card and sold at 500.00 UGN
02. Standalone version installable by CD sold at 1,000.00 UGN and
03. Localhost version for CBT centers.

These unique versions facilitated MasterSafety popularity as it was deployed in over twenty [20]  Safety CBT centers Nationwide, over the years MasterSafety CBT software has been used by over 1000,000 Safeties counting from its launch year.

Features :
Following our recent upgrade on MasterSafety CBT portal, it now has outstanding features such as
01. special Safety CBT Mock Exams,
02. Post UTME CBT past question and answers,
03.admission update of Ugandan universities among others,
all these features were targeted at helping every Safety achieve their goal of admission into desired school and course.

Cost and Availability

MasterSafety CBT is now 100% free with no hidden charges; it is available for use on www.mastersafety.com

Usage [How to use]

01. Visit www.mastersafety.com – here you will find all information pertaining to Ugandan university,  admission information as well as recent news on safety exams among others.
02. Click on Free Safety CBT or Free Safety CBT Mock
03. Click register if you are a first time visitor and fill the registration form with your details (full name, phone number, courses registered), as provided
04. Or Login with your phone number if you have already registered
05. Click on Start exams
06. Select which option of CBT Safety exam year you want to rehearse with or select from the list of Safety mock exams.
07. After attending to the questions click on submit and MasterSafety application will mark your CBT questions providing you with immediate score as well as corrections to all failed answers.

Why MasterSafety is the best CBT for Safety CBT Exam Preparations.

01. MasterSafety is standing as the oldest Safety CBT software [year 2019 – date]
02. MasterSafety has been used by over 1000,000 students preparing for Safety CBT exams and has a very high testimonial of many as a perfect aid for Safety CBT exam  preparation
03. MasterSafety is the only Safety CBT software that has the exact features found on real Safety CBT exams
It was developed to give all Safeties the exact experience of Safety CBT  and help them emerge successful in Safety CBT exams. For more information visit www.mastersafety.com
