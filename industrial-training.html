1 IT / Internship / SIWES

Student Industrial Work Experience Scheme (SIWES) popularly known as Industrial Training [IT] is a program specially structured for undergraduates to allow them the practical work experience they are likely to meet in the labor market after they have graduated from school.
The sole purpose of industrial training is to build in students certain skills and ethics that would make them employable or grant them entrepreneur capacity.
